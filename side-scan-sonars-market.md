<p><p>Side scan sonar technology is categorized into two primary types: single-beam and multi-beam. Single-beam sonar uses one focused beam to map the seafloor, ideal for detailed imaging of specific areas. In contrast, multi-beam sonar employs multiple beams simultaneously, providing a broader coverage area and generating detailed 3D images. Each type serves distinct applications, with single-beam sonar excelling in high-resolution imaging and multi-beam sonar being favored for extensive surveys, making them crucial tools in marine exploration and underwater mapping.</p></p>
