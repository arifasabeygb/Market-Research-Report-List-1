<p><p>The laminated glass market includes several key types based on interlayer materials. PVB (polyvinyl butyral) is the most widely used due to its excellent safety and acoustic properties. EVA (ethylene-vinyl acetate) offers enhanced UV resistance and is favored for solar applications. SGP (SentryGlas®) provides superior strength and durability, making it ideal for high-demand environments. Other types may include specialized interlayers for specific applications, catering to aesthetic, security, and energy efficiency needs. Each type serves distinct market requirements.</p></p>
