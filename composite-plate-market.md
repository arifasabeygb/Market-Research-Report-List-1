<p><strong>The Composite Plate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Explosion Bonded</li><li>Roll Bonded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite plate market consists of various types, including explosion bonded and roll bonded plates. Explosion bonded plates are created by using controlled explosive forces to fuse two or more materials, resulting in a strong bond and excellent resistance to temperature and pressure. In contrast, roll bonded plates are produced by mechanically pressing layers together under heat, yielding a uniform thickness and improved dimensional stability. Both methods cater to industries requiring advanced material properties, such as aerospace, automotive, and military applications.</p></p>
