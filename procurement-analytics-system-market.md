<p><p>Procurement analytics systems are categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise systems are installed locally on a company's servers, providing more control over data security and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Conversely, cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, easier updates, and lower initial costs. This flexibility allows businesses to adapt their procurement strategies more efficiently while leveraging advanced analytics regardless of their IT infrastructure.</p></p>
