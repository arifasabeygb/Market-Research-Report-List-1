<p><p>The Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) equipment market comprises two primary types: sputtering deposition and evaporation deposition. Sputtering deposition involves ejecting material from a target onto a substrate using energetic ions, allowing for precise film thickness control and uniform coatings. In contrast, evaporation deposition involves vaporizing a material and allowing it to condense onto a substrate, commonly used for metals and dielectrics. Both methods are essential in applications like semiconductor manufacturing, optics, and decorative coatings, driven by industry demands for advanced materials.</p></p>
