<p><p>The silicon-based fingerprint sensor market is divided into two main types: touch type and slide type. Touch type sensors capture fingerprints when a user presses their finger on a designated area, providing quick and accurate scans. These are commonly found in smartphones and secure devices. Slide type sensors, on the other hand, require users to slide their fingers across a scanning surface, allowing for continuous capture that can be utilized in various applications, such as access control and time attendance systems.</p></p>
