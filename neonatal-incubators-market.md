<p><p>The neonatal incubator market is primarily divided into two types: transport neonatal incubators and normal neonatal incubators. Transport neonatal incubators are designed for the safe transfer of premature or critically ill infants, providing controlled thermal environments during transit. Normal neonatal incubators, on the other hand, are utilized within healthcare facilities to maintain optimal temperature, humidity, and oxygen levels for stable care. Both types are crucial for ensuring the health and survival of vulnerable newborns in different settings.</p></p>
