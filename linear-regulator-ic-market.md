<p><ul><li>"Series Type"</li><li>"Shunt Type"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear regulator IC market is primarily divided into two types: series type and shunt type. Series type regulators control output voltage by adjusting the resistance in series with the load, ensuring stable voltage even with varying input. In contrast, shunt type regulators manage voltage by diverting excess current, effectively maintaining a constant output. Both types are vital for applications requiring low noise and simplicity, commonly used in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial power supplies to deliver reliable voltage regulation.</p></p>
