<p><strong>The Artificial Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monofocal Artificial Lens</li><li>Multifocal Artificial Lens</li><li>Toric Artificial Lens</li><li>Accommodative Artificial Lens</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial lens market consists of various types catering to different vision correction needs. Monofocal artificial lenses provide a single focus point for clear vision at one distance. Multifocal artificial lenses offer multiple focal points, assisting with near and distance vision. Toric artificial lenses are designed for astigmatism correction, featuring varying powers. Accommodative artificial lenses adjust focus dynamically, mimicking natural eye function. Additionally, the "Others" category includes specialized lenses addressing unique vision requirements, enhancing overall market diversity.</p></p>
