<p><p>OLSO crystallizers can be categorized into vertical and horizontal markets based on their design and application. Vertical crystallizers are designed for efficient space utilization, typically featuring a tall, narrow structure, making them suitable for processes with high sedimentation rates. In contrast, horizontal crystallizers have a wider, flatter design, providing better heat exchange and mixing properties, which is ideal for larger volumes of solution. Both types serve specific industrial needs, ensuring optimal crystallization processes in various applications.</p></p>
