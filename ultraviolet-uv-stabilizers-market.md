<p><p>The Ultraviolet (UV) stabilizers market comprises various types, each serving to enhance material durability against UV radiation. Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS) are highly effective for long-term protection, preventing polymer degradation. UV absorbers absorb UV light and convert it into harmless energy, while quenchers dissipate energy from excited states of molecules, thereby reducing oxidative damage. Other market categories may include specific blends or custom formulations designed for unique applications, ensuring comprehensive protection across different materials and products.</p></p>
