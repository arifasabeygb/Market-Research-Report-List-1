<p><ul><li>Deep Groove Ball Bearing</li><li>Self-aligning Ball Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ball bearings market is primarily characterized by different types, including deep groove ball bearings and self-aligning ball bearings. Deep groove ball bearings are widely used due to their versatility, accommodating radial and axial loads while operating at high speeds. In contrast, self-aligning ball bearings can tolerate misalignment, making them ideal for applications with shaft deflections or misalignment issues. Both types cater to various industries, ensuring smooth and efficient mechanical movement in machinery and equipment.</p></p>
