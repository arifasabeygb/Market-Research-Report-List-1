<p><p>The Online Water Quality Monitoring Analyzer market is categorized into two primary types: single-parameter and multi-parameter measurement. Single-parameter measurement devices assess one specific contaminant or quality indicator, such as pH or turbidity, providing targeted and precise analysis. In contrast, multi-parameter measurement devices analyze several water quality parameters simultaneously, offering a comprehensive overview of water conditions, including temperature, dissolved oxygen, and chemical concentrations. This versatility enhances monitoring capabilities for various applications, including environmental regulations and industrial processes.</p></p>
