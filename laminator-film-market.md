<p><strong>The Laminator Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal</li><li>Low-temp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laminator film market is primarily divided into thermal and low-temperature types. Thermal laminator film requires heat to bond with substrates, providing a durable and high-gloss finish. It is commonly used for enhancing printed materials, such as business cards and brochures. In contrast, low-temperature laminator film adheres without high heat, making it suitable for heat-sensitive materials, including certain inks and papers. This type is essential for applications where heat could distort or damage the substrate.</p></p>
