<p><p>The ball fire extinguisher market primarily includes two types: sand and gravel fire extinguishing bombs, and dry powder fire extinguishing bombs. Sand and gravel extinguishing bombs utilize natural materials to smother flames, effective for certain classes of fires, especially those involving liquids. In contrast, dry powder extinguishing bombs release a powder that interrupts the combustion process, suitable for a wider range of fire classes. Both types are designed for easy deployment, providing proactive fire suppression in various settings.</p></p>
