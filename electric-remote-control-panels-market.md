<p><p>The Electric Remote Control Panels market encompasses two primary types: Low Tension Control Panels and High Tension Control Panels. Low Tension Control Panels manage electrical systems with voltage levels typically below 1 kV, suitable for industrial and residential applications, ensuring efficient operation and safety. In contrast, High Tension Control Panels cater to voltages above 1 kV, essential for managing electrical power in larger installations, such as power plants and substations, facilitating reliable distribution and minimizing risk in high-voltage environments.</p></p>
