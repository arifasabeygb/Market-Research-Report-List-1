<p><p>The UV and Visible Spectroscopy market encompasses various system types tailored to specific applications. Single-beam systems measure absorbance by directing light through the sample, making them ideal for simple tasks. Dual-beam systems offer enhanced accuracy by comparing signals from the sample and a reference beam. Array-based systems facilitate rapid analysis by simultaneously detecting multiple wavelengths, increasing throughput. Handheld systems provide portability and convenience for field measurements, catering to users requiring quick assessments in diverse environments.</p></p>
