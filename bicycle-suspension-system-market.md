<p><p>The bicycle suspension system market primarily features two types: hardtail and full suspension. Hardtail bicycles have a solid rear frame with only front suspension, offering efficiency and lightweight design ideal for climbing and smoother terrains. In contrast, full suspension bikes include both front and rear suspension systems, providing enhanced comfort and control on rough trails by absorbing shocks and bumps. This differentiation caters to varying rider preferences and terrain challenges, influencing market growth and innovation.</p></p>
