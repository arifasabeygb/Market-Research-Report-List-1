<p><strong>The Micronized Calcium Carbonate Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC)</li><li>Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Micronized Calcium Carbonate Powder market is divided into two main types: Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC) and Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC). GCC is derived from natural limestone, ground into a fine powder, and is commonly used in construction, plastics, and coatings due to its bulk and low cost. PCC, on the other hand, is synthetically produced through a chemical process, resulting in finer, purer particles. PCC is preferred in applications requiring high purity and specific properties, such as pharmaceuticals and food.</p></p>
