<p><p>The ICU ECG patient monitor market is divided into three main types: stationary, portable, and implantable. Stationary monitors are typically fixed units used in hospitals for continuous patient monitoring. Portable monitors are lightweight and designed for mobility, allowing for use in various settings, including emergency care and home health. Implantable monitors are surgically placed devices that provide continuous ECG monitoring for long-term assessment of cardiac health, offering a discreet and ongoing solution for patients with specific needs.</p></p>
