<p><strong>The Laser Safety Goggles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective Goggles</li><li>Absorbing Goggles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser safety goggles market features two main types: reflective goggles and absorbing goggles. Reflective goggles are designed to deflect harmful laser radiation away from the eyes using specialized coatings, making them effective for high-intensity lasers. In contrast, absorbing goggles protect by absorbing specific wavelengths of laser light, minimizing transmission to the eyes. Both types are crucial for ensuring safety in environments where lasers are used, such as laboratories, medical facilities, and industrial settings, each providing tailored solutions for different laser applications.</p></p>
