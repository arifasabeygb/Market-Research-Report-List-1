<p><p>The Helicobacter Pylori invasive testing market includes several key types: Histology, Rapid Urease Test (RUT), and HP Culture. Histology involves examining gastric tissue samples microscopically to detect H. pylori. The Rapid Urease Test (RUT) assesses enzymatic activity by placing a biopsy in a urea-containing medium that changes color if H. pylori is present. HP Culture involves growing H. pylori from biopsy samples in culture media to confirm infection. Together, these methods provide vital diagnostic insights for suitable treatment options.</p></p>
