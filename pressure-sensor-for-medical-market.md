<p><p>Pressure sensors in the medical market are essential for monitoring physiological parameters. Single disposable sensors are designed for one-time use, minimizing infection risk. Single pressure line systems measure pressure in a single location, ideal for basic monitoring. Double pressure line sensors allow for simultaneous measurement from two sites, enhancing diagnostic capabilities. Triple pressure line sensors provide even more comprehensive monitoring by measuring pressures from three different sites, beneficial for complex cases and advanced patient management.</p></p>
